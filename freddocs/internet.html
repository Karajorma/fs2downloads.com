Internet Links
 
Note: to follow the links in this section, your computer must be connected to the Internet.

This file points you to Internet resources for getting help, learning more, and sharing missions with others.

FreeSpace 2 was developed by Volition, Inc. and published by Interplay.

The newsgroup alt.games.descent discusses both the Descent and FreeSpace series. If you want to find the real FRED gurus, though, join the FreeSpace Developer List (FDL). The folks on this mailing list will provide you with quick answers and all kinds of new ideas. There's also a FreeSpace Universe Discussion List (FUDL) covering physics, history, ships, and so on.

There are many great fan sites out there. The Descent Chronicles, covering both the Descent and FreeSpace series, features an extensive mission archive (with reviews) and a webring of other FreeSpace 2 sites. The FreeSpace SwapMeet is chock full of useful advice, and it can connect you with other designers willing to swap voice acting (for .wav files).
